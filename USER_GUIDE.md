## Welcome to AutoWash Hub

AutoWash Hub is your complete car wash management solution that makes booking and managing car wash appointments simple and convenient. Our platform allows you to:

- **Book appointments** quickly and easily
- **Track your bookings** in real-time
- **Manage multiple vehicles** in one place
- **View transaction history** and download invoices
- **Receive notifications** about your appointments
- **Access services** from any device

### System Requirements

- **Web Browser:** Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge (latest versions)

- **Device:** Desktop, laptop, tablet, or smartphone

1. Open your web browser
2. Navigate to the AutoWash Hub website

## Account Management

### Creating Your Account

#### Step 1: Registration

1. Click the **"Sign Up"** or **"Register"** button on the landing page
2. Fill in the registration form:
   - **First Name:** Your first name
   - **Last Name:** Your last name
   - **Email Address:** A valid email address
   - **Phone Number:** Your contact number (format: 09XXXXXXXXX)
   - **Password:** Create a strong password (minimum 8 characters, include uppercase, number, and special character)
   - **Confirm Password:** Re-enter your password
3. Click **"Register"** to create your account

#### Step 2: Email Verification

1. Check your email inbox for a verification email from AutoWash Hub
2. Click the verification link in the email
3. Your account will be activated immediately
4. You can now log in to your account

> **Note:** The verification email should arrive within 5 seconds. Check your spam folder if you don't see it.

### Logging In

1. Click **"Login"** on the landing page
2. Enter your **email address**
3. Enter your **password**
4. Click **"Login"**
5. You'll be redirected to your customer dashboard

### Forgot Password?

If you forget your password:

1. Click **"Forgot Password?"** on the login page
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Click **"Send Reset Link"**
4. Check your email for the password reset link
5. Click the link in the email
6. Enter your new password
7. Confirm your new password
8. Click **"Reset Password"**
9. Log in with your new password

> **Note:** Password reset links are valid for 24 hours.

### Logging Out

To log out of your account:

1. Click on your **profile icon** or **username** in the navigation bar
2. Select **"Logout"** from the dropdown menu
3. You'll be redirected to the landing page

## Booking an Appointment

### Overview of the Booking Process

Booking a car wash appointment is easy and takes just a few minutes:

1. Select date and time
2. Choose your service package
3. Select or add your vehicle
4. Review and confirm
5. Make payment
6. Receive confirmation

### Step-by-Step Booking Guide

#### Step 1: Start a New Booking

1. Log in to your account
2. Click **"Book Appointment"** or **"New Booking"** from your dashboard
3. The booking form will open

#### Step 2: Select Date and Time

1. **Choose a Date:**
   - Use the calendar to select your preferred date
   - Available dates are highlighted
   - Past dates are disabled
2. **Choose a Time Slot:**
   - View available time slots for your selected date
   - Booked slots will be grayed out or disabled
   - Select an available time slot

> **Tip:** Book early to get your preferred time slot, especially on weekends!

#### Step 3: Choose Your Service Package

Select from our available service packages:

##### Basic Wash

- Exterior hand wash
- Tire cleaning
- Window cleaning
- **Price:** $15-$25 (depending on vehicle type)
- **Duration:** ~30 minutes

##### Wash + Vacuum

- Everything in Basic Wash
- Interior vacuuming
- Dashboard cleaning
- **Price:** $25-$35 (depending on vehicle type)
- **Duration:** ~45 minutes

##### Premium Wash + Wax

- Everything in Wash + Vacuum
- Hand wax application
- Tire shine
- Interior detailing
- **Price:** $35-$50 (depending on vehicle type)
- **Duration:** ~60 minutes

> **Note:** Prices vary based on vehicle type (Sedan, SUV, Truck, Van)

#### Step 4: Select Your Vehicle

**Option A: Select an Existing Vehicle**

- Choose from your saved vehicles
- Vehicle details will be pre-filled

**Option B: Add a New Vehicle**

1. Click **"Add New Vehicle"**
2. Enter vehicle details:
   - **Make:** (e.g., Toyota, Honda, Ford)
   - **Model:** (e.g., Camry, Civic, F-150)
   - **Year:** (e.g., 2023)
   - **Color:** (e.g., White, Black, Blue)
   - **License Plate:** (e.g., ABC123)
3. Click **"Save Vehicle"**
4. The vehicle will be added to your profile for future bookings

#### Step 5: Review Your Booking

Review all booking details:

- **Date & Time:** Confirm your appointment time
- **Service Package:** Verify the selected service
- **Vehicle:** Check vehicle information
- **Total Price:** Review the total cost

#### Step 6: Confirm and Pay

1. Review the **Terms & Conditions**
2. Check the box to agree
3. Click **"Confirm Booking"**
4. You'll be directed to the payment page

#### Step 7: Make Payment

1. Select your payment method:
   - Credit/Debit Card
   - Other available payment options
2. Enter payment details
3. Click **"Pay Now"**
4. Wait for payment confirmation

#### Step 8: Booking Confirmation

After successful payment:

- You'll see a confirmation message with your **Booking ID**
- A confirmation email will be sent to your registered email
- The booking will appear in your dashboard
- Your appointment is now confirmed!

## Managing Your Bookings

### Viewing Your Appointments

1. Log in to your account
2. Navigate to **"My Appointments"** or **"My Bookings"**
3. You'll see a list of all your bookings

**Booking Information Displayed:**

- Booking ID
- Date and time
- Service package
- Vehicle details
- Status (Pending, Confirmed, In Progress, Completed, Cancelled)
- Total amount

### Understanding Booking Status

| Status              | Description                               |
| ------------------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| **Pending Payment** | Booking created but payment not completed |
| **Confirmed**       | Payment received, appointment confirmed   |
| **In Progress**     | Your vehicle is currently being serviced  |
| **Completed**       | Service finished successfully             |
| **Cancelled**       | Appointment was cancelled                 |
| **Rescheduled**     | Appointment was moved to a different time |

### Viewing Appointment Details

1. Go to **"My Appointments"**
2. Click on any booking to view full details
3. You'll see:
   - Complete booking information
   - Status timeline
   - Assigned employee (if available)
   - Estimated completion time
   - Payment details

### Cancelling an Appointment

To cancel a booking:

1. Go to **"My Appointments"**
2. Find the appointment you want to cancel
3. Click **"Cancel"** button
4. Confirm the cancellation
5. Optionally provide a reason for cancellation
6. Click **"Confirm Cancellation"**

**Cancellation Policy:**

- Cancellations must be made at least **24 hours** before the appointment
- Refunds will be processed according to our refund policy
- You'll receive a cancellation confirmation email
- The time slot will become available for other customers

> **Important:** Late cancellations (within 24 hours) may not be eligible for a full refund.

### Rescheduling an Appointment

To reschedule a booking:

1. Go to **"My Appointments"**
2. Find the appointment you want to reschedule
3. Click **"Reschedule"** button
4. Select a new date and time
5. Review any rescheduling fees (if applicable)
6. Click **"Confirm Reschedule"**
7. Your appointment will be updated

**Rescheduling Notes:**

- Original booking will be marked as "Rescheduled"
- New booking will be created with updated details
- You'll receive a confirmation email with new details
- No additional service charges (only rescheduling fee if applicable)

## Payments & Transactions

### Viewing Transaction History

1. Log in to your account
2. Navigate to **"Transaction History"** or **"Billing"**
3. You'll see all your past transactions

**Transaction Information:**

- Transaction ID
- Date and time
- Booking reference
- Service details
- Amount paid
- Payment method
- Status

### Filtering Transactions

You can filter your transactions by:

- **Date Range:** Select start and end dates
- **Status:** Completed, Pending, Refunded
- **Amount:** Sort by amount
- **Payment Method:** Filter by payment type

### Downloading Invoices

To download a receipt or invoice:

1. Go to **"Transaction History"**
2. Find the transaction
3. Click **"Download Invoice"** or **"Download Receipt"**
4. A PDF file will be downloaded to your device

**Invoice Contents:**

- Invoice number
- Date of service
- Customer information
- Service details
- Vehicle information
- Amount breakdown
- Payment method
- Company information

### Understanding Refunds

If you cancel an appointment:

- Refunds are processed according to our cancellation policy
- Refund status will be shown in transaction history
- Refunds typically take 5-7 business days to appear in your account
- You'll receive an email notification when the refund is processed

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### I didn't receive the verification email

**Solutions:**

1. Check your spam or junk folder
2. Wait a few minutes (emails can take up to 5 minutes)
3. Click "Resend Verification Email" on the login page
4. Ensure you entered the correct email address
5. Contact support if the issue persists

#### I can't log in to my account

**Solutions:**

1. Verify you're using the correct email address
2. Check if Caps Lock is on (passwords are case-sensitive)
3. Try resetting your password using "Forgot Password"
4. Clear your browser cache and cookies
5. Try a different browser
6. Ensure your account is verified

#### I don't see any available time slots

**Possible Reasons:**

1. The selected date is fully booked
2. You're trying to book a past date
3. The business may be closed on that day
4. Try selecting a different date
5. Book further in advance for better availability

#### My payment failed

**Solutions:**

1. Check your card details are correct
2. Ensure you have sufficient funds
3. Verify your card is not expired
4. Try a different payment method
5. Contact your bank if the issue persists
6. Contact our support team for assistance

#### I can't cancel my appointment

**Possible Reasons:**

1. The appointment is within 24 hours (cancellation deadline passed)
2. The appointment is already in progress or completed
3. Technical issue - try refreshing the page
4. Contact support for urgent cancellations

#### The website is loading slowly

**Solutions:**

1. Check your internet connection
2. Clear browser cache and cookies
3. Try a different browser
4. Disable browser extensions temporarily
5. Try accessing from a different device
6. Check if the website is under maintenance

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Account & Registration

**Q: Is registration free?**  
A: Yes, creating an account on AutoWash Hub is completely free.

**Q: Can I use the same email for multiple accounts?**  
A: No, each email address can only be associated with one account.

**Q: How do I verify my email?**  
A: Click the verification link sent to your email after registration.

### Booking & Appointments

**Q: How far in advance can I book?**  
A: You can typically book appointments up to 30 days in advance.

**Q: Can I book multiple appointments at once?**  
A: Yes, you can create multiple bookings for different dates and vehicles.

**Q: What if I'm late for my appointment?**  
A: Please contact us immediately. Late arrivals may result in shortened service time or rescheduling.

**Q: Can someone else bring my car for the appointment?**  
A: Yes, but please inform us in advance and provide their contact information.

**Q: Do you offer same-day appointments?**  
A: Subject to availability. Check the booking system for open slots.

### Services & Pricing

**Q: What's included in each service package?**  
A: See the "Choose Your Service Package" section for detailed descriptions.

**Q: Do prices vary by vehicle type?**  
A: Yes, larger vehicles (SUVs, trucks, vans) have higher prices than sedans.

**Q: Are there any additional fees?**  
A: Prices shown include all standard services. Additional services may incur extra charges.

**Q: Do you offer discounts or loyalty programs?**  
A: Check your dashboard or contact us for current promotions and loyalty programs.

### Payments & Refunds

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**  
A: We accept major credit/debit cards and other payment methods shown at checkout.

**Q: When will I be charged?**  
A: Payment is processed immediately after you confirm your booking.

**Q: How long do refunds take?**  
A: Refunds typically take 5-7 business days to appear in your account.

**Q: Can I get a receipt for my payment?**  
A: Yes, you can download invoices from your transaction history.

### Cancellations & Rescheduling

**Q: What is your cancellation policy?**  
A: Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours before the appointment for a full refund.

**Q: Is there a fee for rescheduling?**  
A: Rescheduling policies vary. Check during the rescheduling process for any applicable fees.

**Q: How many times can I reschedule?**  
A: There's typically no limit, but excessive rescheduling may be subject to review.

### Technical Issues

**Q: Which browsers are supported?**  
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions recommended).

**Q: Can I use the system on my mobile phone?**  
A: Yes, AutoWash Hub is fully responsive and works on all devices.

**Q: Is my payment information secure?**  
A: Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to protect your payment data.
